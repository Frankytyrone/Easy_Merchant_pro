1. Logging In

Open your web browser (Chrome or Safari recommended).
Go to the app URL provided by your manager.
Enter your Username and Password.
Click Login. You will see the New Invoice screen.
💡 Tip: On a phone or tablet, tap the menu icon in your browser and select Add to Home Screen to install the app so it opens like a normal app.
⚠️ Never share your password with anyone. If you think someone else knows it, ask your manager to reset it immediately.

2. Daily Operations Overview

When you start your shift:

  1. Log in as described above.
  2. Check the store selector at the top of the screen — make sure it shows the correct store (Falcarragh or Gweedore).
  3. Check the connection indicator — 🟢 Online means you are connected to the server. 🔴 Offline means you are working locally — see Section 10.

When you finish your shift:

  1. Make sure all invoices are saved (not just drafts).
  2. Click your username in the top right → Logout.

3. Creating & Managing Invoices

3.1 How to Create a New Invoice

Click ➕ New Invoice in the left sidebar (or bottom bar on mobile).
Search for the customer in the Customer box, or click 💵 Cash Sale for a walk-in customer.
Check the Invoice Date and Due Date are correct.
Search for products in the Product Search box (see Section 4) or scan a barcode (see Section 5).
Adjust Qty (quantity) and Disc% (discount) for each line if needed.
Click 💾 Save Invoice.
💡 The invoice number is generated automatically in the format FAL-XXXXX (Falcarragh) or GWE-XXXXX (Gweedore).

3.2 How to Apply a Discount

On each invoice line, there is a Disc% column. Type the discount percentage (e.g. 10 for 10% off). The totals update automatically.

3.3 How to Change VAT Rate

Each line has a VAT% column. Common rates are:

RateWhen to use
23%Standard rate — most hardware, tools, materials
0%Zero-rated — certain animal feeds, seeds, fertilisers
9%Reduced rate — not common for this type of business

Click on the VAT% cell to change it. The line total will recalculate.

3.4 How to Print an Invoice

Save the invoice first.
Click 🖨️ Print PDF.
A PDF opens — use your browser's print dialog to print it, or save it.

3.5 How to Email an Invoice

Make sure the customer has an email address saved (visible in the customer details).
Click 📧 Email.
Confirm the email address shown and click Send.
The invoice status shows Sent. When the customer opens it, it shows Opened.

3.6 How to Send a Payment Link

Open the saved invoice.
Click 💳 Send Payment Link.
A payment link is generated. Copy it and send it to the customer by email, WhatsApp, or text.
When the customer pays online, the invoice is automatically marked as Paid.

3.7 How to Make a Mistake — Edit an Invoice

🚫 You cannot edit a paid invoice. If an invoice is already paid and is wrong, you must cancel it and create a new one. See below.
Find the invoice in the Invoices list.
Open the invoice by clicking on it.
If it is not paid: make your changes and click Save.
If it is paid: click Cancel Invoice and confirm. Then create a new correct invoice.

4. Products & Searching

4.1 How to Search for a Product (Typing)

In the invoice editor, click the product search box and start typing:

  • You can type the product code (e.g. TIM001).
  • You can type part of the description (e.g. nail finds "Steel Nails 60mm").
  • You can type the barcode number.

Results appear as a dropdown after a short pause. Click a result to add it to the invoice.

💡 Use arrow keys ↑↓ to navigate the results and Enter to select without touching the mouse.

5. Barcode Scanning

5.1 Using a USB or Bluetooth Scanner

These scanners act like a keyboard — they "type" the barcode number for you.

Click on the product search box in the invoice editor.
Scan the barcode with the scanner.
The barcode number is entered automatically. If the product is found, it is added to the invoice.

5.2 Using the Camera on a Phone or Tablet

In the invoice editor, tap the 📷 camera icon next to the product search box.
Point your camera at the barcode on the product.
The app reads the barcode and adds the product automatically.
💡 Make sure you allow the browser to use your camera when it asks. You only need to do this once.
⚠️ Product not found? If the barcode scan returns "Product not found", search manually by typing the product name. The product may not have a barcode set up in the system yet — contact your manager.

6. Customers

6.1 How to Find an Existing Customer

In the Customer search box at the top of the invoice, type the customer's name, phone number, or email. Results appear as a dropdown.

6.2 How to Add a New Customer

Click ➕ Add New Customer in the invoice editor (or go to the Customers screen).
Fill in the customer details. Company Name is required.
Click Save Customer. The customer is now linked to the invoice.

6.3 How to Check Outstanding Payments

Go to the Customers screen.
Search for or click on the customer.
You will see all their invoices and outstanding balances.

7. Taking Payments

7.1 How to Take a Cash Payment

Open the invoice.
Click 💵 Add Payment.
Select Cash from the payment method dropdown.
Enter the amount received.
Click Save Payment.

7.2 How to Take a Card Payment

Process the payment on your card terminal as normal.
Once the terminal shows approved, open the invoice in EBM Pro.
Click Add Payment, select Credit/Debit Card, enter the amount, click Save Payment.

8. Quotes

8.1 How to Create a Quote

Click ➕ New Invoice.
At the top of the invoice editor, click the Quote button (to the right of "Invoice").
Add customer, products, and details as normal.
Click Save Quote. The number will be in the format FAL-Q-XXXX.

8.2 How to Convert a Quote to an Invoice

Find the quote in the Invoices list (filter by type: Quote).
Open the quote.
Click Convert to Invoice.
A new invoice is created with the same items. The quote is marked as converted.

9. Reports

Click 📊 Reports in the sidebar.
Select the date range and store.
View totals for sales, VAT, payments received, and outstanding balances.
Click Export to download as a spreadsheet if needed.

End of Day Report

Run the Reports section at the end of each day to see total takings by payment method. Print or save this as a daily record.

10. Working Offline

The app works even without an internet connection.

ℹ️ When offline, you will see a red 🔴 Offline indicator at the top of the screen and an orange "OFFLINE MODE" banner.

While offline you can:

  • Create new invoices (saved locally on your device)
  • Search products already loaded
  • Add payments

When your internet connection comes back, the app automatically syncs everything saved offline. You will see a confirmation message "X items synced".

⚠️ Important: Do not clear your browser data while offline — you may lose unsynchronised invoices. Wait until you see "🟢 Online" before clearing history.

11. If Something Goes Wrong

Internet Goes Off

The app will switch to offline mode automatically. Keep working as normal. When internet comes back, everything syncs automatically.

The App is Slow

  • Try refreshing the page (press F5 or pull down to refresh on mobile).
  • If still slow, close other browser tabs.
  • If it remains slow, report it to your manager — it may be a server issue.

A Product Won't Scan

  • Make sure the barcode is clean and not damaged.
  • Try scanning again from a slightly different angle or distance.
  • If still not working, search manually by typing the product name or code.
  • If the product should be in the system, report the missing barcode to the manager so it can be added.

Made a Mistake on an Invoice

  • If the invoice is not paid: open it, make changes, save.
  • If the invoice is paid: cancel the invoice and create a new correct one. Never alter a paid invoice.

Forgot Password

Ask your manager or admin to reset your password in the Admin Dashboard.

12. Admin Only

⚠️ This section is for admins only. Standard operators will not see these options.

12.1 How to Add a New Operator

Go to the Admin Dashboard (top-right menu → Admin, or navigate to /ebmpro/admin.html).
Click the Operators tab.
Click ➕ Add Operator.
Enter username, role (Counter / Manager / Admin), store, and password.
Click Save.

12.2 How to Reset a Password

Go to Admin Dashboard → Operators tab.
Find the operator and click Reset PW.
Enter the new password (minimum 8 characters).
Tell the operator their new password in person — never by email or text.

12.3 How to Download a Backup

Go to Admin Dashboard → Backups tab.
Click ⬇️ Download next to any backup to save it to your computer.
Or click 💾 Create Backup Now to generate a fresh backup immediately.
ℹ️ Backups are also created automatically every night at midnight. The last 30 backups are kept. Store a copy off-site (e.g. USB drive or cloud storage) for safety.

12.4 How to Import Products / Customers

Export your data from the Easy Invoicing Access database as XML or CSV.
Go to Admin Dashboard → Import tab.
Choose the correct import type (Products, Customers, Invoices, or Payments).
Click Choose File and select your exported file.
Click Import. A progress bar shows the import status.
When complete, a summary shows how many records were inserted, skipped (duplicates), or had errors.
💡 The import is safe to run multiple times — it will not create duplicate products/customers. Existing records (matched by product code or legacy ID) are skipped.
